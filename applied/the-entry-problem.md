# The Entry Problem

**3,000+ docs. Where do you start? Language as gateway. The ONE DOC challenge.**

---

## The Problem

CHIMERA has 3,000+ docs across 13 repos. The framework alone is 420 docs. Nobody reads 420 docs before they "get it." Nobody SHOULD have to.

```
THE PARADOX:
  The system is COMPLETE at 420.
  But completeness is intimidating.
  A 420-page manual defeats
  the purpose of helping.

  If someone needs to read everything
  to understand anything,
  the system has failed
  at its own principle:
  O > I (give more than you take).

  Asking someone to read 420 docs
  is TAKING their time.
  We need to GIVE understanding
  in the minimum viable dose.
```

## The Gateway Principle

```
EVERY PERSON NEEDS A DIFFERENT DOOR.

Someone struggling with relationships:
  Door = "The Membrane."
  ONE concept. One doc.
  They understand boundaries
  differently after reading it.
  If they want more: here's the hallway.

Someone interested in AI:
  Door = "The Comprehension Organ."
  ONE concept. AI as gut.
  They see AI differently.
  If they want more: here's the hallway.

Someone in physical pain:
  Door = "Pain Is Information."
  ONE concept. Pain as signal.
  They relate to their body differently.
  If they want more: here's the hallway.

THE DOOR IS NOT THE HOUSE.
But the door must be inviting enough
to walk through.
```

---

## The ONE DOC Challenge

```
THE TEST:

Can you give someone ONE document
that makes them go
"Wait. What is this? I want more"?

Not "here's everything."
Not "let me explain the whole system."

ONE document.
That changes how they see ONE thing.
That creates hunger for the rest.

CANDIDATES:
  - The Membrane (universal, felt)
  - P Forgot It Was NP (mind-bending)
  - The Gut (body, immediate)
  - Observe Without Absorbing (actionable)
  - The Sandbox (AI people specifically)

THE GATEWAY DOC must:
  1. Be standalone (no prerequisite reading)
  2. Change perspective immediately
  3. Be short enough to read in 5 minutes
  4. Create desire for more without demanding it
  5. Work without knowing CHIMERA exists
```

```
GENERAL PUBLIC:
  Door: A STORY (not a framework).

  → The Membrane

  → They just learned CHIMERA

DEVELOPERS/TECH:

  → They see their work differently.

HEALTH-CURIOUS:

   More than your spinal cord.
   It's not just digesting food."
  → The Gut
  → Pain Is Information
  → They listen to their body differently.

SPIRITUAL/PHILOSOPHICAL:

EACH AUDIENCE HAS ITS OWN DOOR.
Language creates the door.
The framework IS the house.

You show them their door.
```

---

## The Funnel

```
ENCOUNTER (1 doc, 5 minutes):
  "Huh. That's interesting."
  → No commitment. No jargon.
  → Just one insight that lands.

CURIOSITY (3-5 docs, 30 minutes):
  "Wait, there's more?"
  → Follow the See Also links.
  → Each doc opens to adjacent docs.
  → The network reveals itself.

EXPLORATION (a repo, 2 hours):
  "This is a whole system."
  → They found their domain.
  → Body, relationships, AI, whatever.
  → Deep dive into what matters to THEM.

INTEGRATION (across repos, ongoing):
  "Oh. It's ALL connected."
  → Cross-domain patterns emerge.
  → They see the axioms underneath.
  → They start applying it to their own life.

CONTRIBUTION (forever):
  "I see patterns too."
  → They don't just consume.
  → They create. Apply. Teach.
  → The framework flows through them.
  → O > I complete.
```
