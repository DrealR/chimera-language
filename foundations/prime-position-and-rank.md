# Prime, Position, and Rank

“Prime” accumulated several meanings inside CHIMERA. Durable language requires separating them.

## The collision

| Historical phrase | Meaning it carried |
|-------------------|--------------------|
| **Prime body** | A smallest functionally irreducible body at a named scale. |
| **Relational prime** | A developmental name for a composite body emerging through relation. |
| **A person's prime** | A proposed vector of current skill coordinates. |
| **Band Prime** | A memorable name for the complete group map. |

These cannot all function as one technical definition.

## Current language

- Use **prime body** only with an explicit scale and irreducible function.
- Replace the older composite usage with **emergent composite body**.
- Use **member position vector** for one participant's current coordinates.
- Use **group position matrix** for the collection of those vectors.
- Use **relationship field** for what participants make possible or difficult for one another.
- Keep **Band Prime** as a carrier name for the complete temporary configuration, never as a mathematical claim or human ranking.

## Position is not identity

A position sentence must name:

```text
body + mission + dimension + evidence + time + uncertainty
```

Prefer:

> “In today's beginner arrangement, the evidence suggests you can hold the pulse independently and are still learning recovery after a missed entry.”

Avoid:

> “You are a level 2 musician.”

The first describes a changing relation. The second cools a temporary observation into identity.

## Rank is a separate operation

Measurement locates. Ranking orders. Selection grants or withholds opportunity. These operations require different justification.

A group may map positions to arrange support without ranking people publicly. If a consequential selection is necessary, state:

- the actual purpose;
- the dimensions used and omitted;
- the evidence and uncertainty;
- the accessibility accommodations;
- who can appeal or correct the record;
- when the decision expires;
- which opportunities remain open.

Do not smuggle ranking into the word “prime.”

## Translation table

| Audience | Safer wording |
|----------|---------------|
| **Child** | “This is what feels easy today, what you are practicing, and who can help.” |
| **Coach** | “Current position, evidence, next bridge, and remap date.” |
| **Builder** | “Time-bound state vector plus interaction graph; no identity inference.” |
| **Artist** | “Your place in this arrangement, not the limit of your sound.” |
| **Public carrier** | “The Band Prime shows the group as it is playing now.” |
| **Expert / skeptic** | “A provisional group-mapping heuristic without psychometric validation.” |

## Carry line

> **A coordinate says where a body is in this arrangement now. It does not say what the person is, what they are worth, or where they can go next.**
